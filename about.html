Spoken is a lighting pattern that uses morse code to display names on the Burning Man 2018 Galaxia Temple - [templegalaxia.org]

Visitors with names they wish to be spoken can submit names online in advance of Burning Man, or on-playa using a terminal near the Temple.

When a light shimmers like a star amongst a galaxy, someone is having their name spoken by the Temple.
